GAAPLogic is a set of decision tools to help you apply some of the most complex accounting guidance ever conceived. We believe that the application of accounting rules should not be the private domain of very expensive accounting firms and subject matter experts. Application of GAAP is usually a methodical process that lends itself to decision analysis. GAAPLogic is just that, a decision analysis toolset designed to save you time and money while applying GAAP to even the most complex transactions.
